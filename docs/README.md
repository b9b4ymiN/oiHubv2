## üéØ Success Tips

### Before You Start:
1. **Don't skip documentation** - 75 min reading saves weeks of losses
2. **Print the Cheat Sheet** - Keep it visible while trading
3. **Paper trade first** - At least 10 trades before going live
4. **Start small** - Use minimum position size initially
5. **Follow rules strictly** - No improvising until you're profitable

### While Trading:
1. **Trust the system** - It's designed to protect you
2. **Don't override position sizing** - Especially on dangerous signals

4. **Multi-timeframe confirmation** - Check at least 2 timeframes

1. **Review trades** - Compare against documentation
2. **Track statistics** - Win rate, R:R, mistakes
